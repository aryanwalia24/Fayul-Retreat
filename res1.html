Hotel Policy & Booking Conditions

Cancelling policy

20% of the booking amount is non-refundable.

We have a credit policy on cancellation wherein your applicable amount stays with us as credit for a period of one year from the original date of booking. There is no cash or bank refund policy on any of your bookings.

-Cancellation before 30 days from check-in date: 80% Credits ( 80% of the booking amount)
-Cancellation between 16 to 30 days from check-in date: 75% Credits (75% booking amount)
-Cancellation between 15-8 days from check-in date: 50%
-Cancellation within 7 days from check-in date: No Credits

You will be able to use your credits for any future single booking for dates within one year from the time of the original booking. These credits are transferable. So, in case you cannot plan for a stay, the credits can be passed on to friends or family. The credits will have to be utilized within a single booking and are applicable only on stays. These credits cannot be used to avail adjustments or discounts on meals. If the credit amount remains unused after a re-booking, or after one year from the original date of booking, it will be treated as lapsed credit. Please note that the credit amount is not refundable in cash and will be used only as a credited amount for future bookings. The actual stay price during the future booking dates may vary depending on the season. All booking requests will be confirmed at a 100% advance payment at the time of booking. In case there is a difference in the amount of your future booking and the credited amount, you would need to transfer the balance to our accounts. In case you reduce your stay, the same cancellation policy applies. If the property cannot host you due to any local or subjective circumstances, we will provide you with a credit of the same amount. You will be able to avail the credited amount for any future booking at Fayul Retreat Kinnaur. For any other queries, please reach out to us at hello fayulretreat@gmail.com.

Any traveler found violating the guidelines shall be dealt with strict actions by the authorities. The right to admission is reserved by Fayul Retreat Kinnaur. Our dormitory shall function with limited occupancy and scattered bed allotment to maintain social distancing. Travelers shall carry their own soaps and towels to maintain hygiene. If you are traveling in a group, you can book a group dorm to avoid contact with outsiders. The entire group shall be allowed to stay in the same dorm in this case.
